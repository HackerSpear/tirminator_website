1. Privacy Policies

Privacy Policy for TIRminator Mobile Application

Effective Date:

This Privacy Policy describes how “TIRminator”, collects, uses, and discloses personal information when you use our Android mobile application for hiring truck drivers in the European region. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. By using our App, you agree to the collection and use of your personal information as outlined in this Policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information from Company Representatives:

When a company representative creates a company profile on our App, we may collect the following personal information:

  • Name and Contact Details: We collect your name, email address, and phone number to identify and communicate with you effectively

  • Company Information: We collect information about your company, including its name, address, industry, and other details necessary to create and manage the company profile.

  • User Preferences and Settings: We may collect information about your preferences and settings within the App to provide a personalized experience.

2.2 Personal Information from Truck Drivers:

When a truck driver creates a profile to search for job opportunities, we may collect the following personal information:

  • Name and Contact Details: We collect your name, email address, and phone number to identify and communicate with you effectively

  • Employment History and Qualifications: We collect information about your employment history, qualifications, and relevant experience to assess your eligibility for job opportunities and facilitate the hiring process.

  • Driving License and Certifications: We may collect information about your driving license, certifications, and other necessary documents to verify your qualifications as a truck driver.

  • User Preferences and Settings: We may collect information about your preferences and settings within the App to provide a personalized experience and match you with relevant job opportunities.

3. Use of Personal Information

3.1 Company Representatives:

We may use the personal information provided by company representatives for the following purposes:

  • Creating and Managing Company Profiles: We use your information to create and manage company profiles, allowing you to post job opportunities and communicate with truck drivers.

  • Facilitating Communication: We may use your personal information to facilitate communication between you and truck drivers who express interest in your job opportunities.

  • Customer Support: We may use your information to provide customer support and address any inquiries or concerns you may have.

  • Sending Updates and Notifications: We may use your contact details to send you relevant updates, notifications, or promotional materials related to our services.

3.2 Truck Drivers:

We may use the personal information provided by truck drivers for the following purposes:

  • Creating and Managing Driver Profiles: We use your information to create and manage driver profiles, allowing you to search for job opportunities and communicate with hiring companies.

  • Matching Drivers with Job Opportunities: We may use your personal information, such as employment history and qualifications, to match you with relevant job opportunities that align with your skills and preferences.

  • Facilitating Communication: We may use your personal information to facilitate communication between you and hiring companies for job-related purposes.

  • Sending Job-Related Updates and Notifications: We may use your contact details to send you updates, notifications, or promotional materials related to job opportunities or our services.

4. Disclosure of Personal Information

4.1 Company Representatives:

We may disclose the personal information of company representatives in the following circumstances:

  • Truck Drivers and Hiring Companies: We may disclose your personal information to truck drivers and hiring companies for job-related purposes, such as facilitating communication and assessing eligibility for job opportunities.

  • Third-Party Service Providers: We may disclose your personal information to third-party service providers who assist us in App operations, such as hosting, payment processing, data analysis, or customer support. These service providers are contractually bound to handle your information securely and only for the purposes specified by us.

  • Legal and Regulatory Authorities: We may disclose your personal information if required or permitted by law, such as to comply with a legal obligation, protect our rights or the safety of others, or respond to lawful requests from public authorities.

4.2 Truck Drivers:

We may disclose the personal information of truck drivers in the following circumstances:

  • Hiring Companies: We may disclose your personal information to company representatives and hiring companies for job-related purposes, such as facilitating communication and assessing eligibility for job opportunities.

  • Third-Party Service Providers: We may disclose your personal information to third-party service providers who assist us in App operations, such as hosting, payment processing, data analysis, or customer support. These service providers are contractually bound to handle your information securely and only for the purposes specified by us.

  • Legal and Regulatory Authorities: We may disclose your personal information if required or permitted by law, such as to comply with a legal obligation, protect our rights or the safety of others, or respond to lawful requests from public authorities.
