The App shall include a rating system that allows users to give ratings to other users based on their collaboration experiences.

How do you assess a driver's level of experience and technical skills in road transport?

(a)How satisfied are you with the driver, given the lack of major incidents such as theft, lorry abandonment, illegal traffic or accidents?

(b) How satisfied are you with the driver's cooperation with the dispatchers and adherence to the work schedule?.

(c) For Users who are natural person, the questions shall be as follows:

How satisfied were you with the working conditions offered by the transport company?.
